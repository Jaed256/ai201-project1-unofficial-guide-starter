TITLE: Ten Iconic Food Spots You Need To Try Around the MMC and BBC Campuses
AUTHOR: Veronica Carbonell, Contributing Writer
DATE: 2021-08-31

Hey Panthers! With new and returning students heading to campus for the Fall semester, we wanted to offer some great places to eat near both the MMC and BBC campuses. Miami has some of the best food in the world, but who knew that so much of it is this close to campus?

Around MMC:

1. 107 Taste FIU (1679 SW 107th Ave): Looking for delicious Pan-Asian cuisine that's just a walk away from campus? Open daily with closing hours ranging from 10 p.m. to 10:30 p.m, 107 Taste FIU has proven itself to be a true lunch and dinner time staple for FIU students alike. With a menu that includes kimchi at $6, steamed bao buns at $10, and more ramen than you can count ranging from $12.50-$13.50; 107 Taste is truly any foodie's dream.

2. specialTEA Lounge and Café (10766 Coral Way): Serving the FIU community for over 12 years, specialTEA is the perfect place for students to recharge with their favorite cup of coffee or tea. Also smoothies, boba teas, and frozen crémes that are perfect for those long days of studying in GL. Open daily from 11 a.m. to 11 p.m. except Sundays when they close at 8 p.m. Their savory menu gives you the option of a panino, salad, wrap, or bowl — all can be substituted with meatless options. Savory items range from $7.50-$9.50 and hot tea can be as low as $2.25.

3. Jasmine's Sushi and Thai Cuisine (1429 SW 107th Ave): Located just across from campus on 107th and open Wednesdays to Sundays from 12 p.m. to 10 p.m, Jasmine's serves a variety of sushi rolls, noodles, pad thai, and curry. Sushi rolls range from $7.00 to $13.95 and lunch entrees range from $9.25 to $13.25. Japanese dinner menu ranges from $14.95 to $18.95.

4. 109 Burger Joint (646 SW 109th Ave): Located directly across the street from 109 Tower. Open from 12 p.m. to 7 p.m. Sundays, until 10 p.m. Monday-Thursday, and midnight on weekends. Signature burgers range from $11.00 to $16.00, including the Mac Addict (mac and cheese) and BBQ Blues. Also a chicken waffle sandwich for $14.00 and a meatball sub for $13.00, with crispy tater tots.

5. Night Owl (10534 SW 8th St): Open Sunday-Thursday until 2 a.m. and Friday-Saturday until 3 a.m, Night Owl is the perfect late night bite for any college student with a sweet tooth. 12 signature cookie flavors including Cinnamon Toast Crunch, Birthday Cake, Vegan Chocolate Chip and their famous cookie shot. Cookie prices range from $2.99 to $3.50.

Near BBC:

6. Paquito's (16265 Biscayne Blvd): Classic Mexican dishes, open daily with closing hours from 10 p.m. to midnight. Tacos starting at $4.25, enchiladas verdes $5.50-$6.50, chiles rellenos at $14.95 and tamales at $12.95.

7. La Birra Bar-North Miami (14831 Biscayne Blvd): Open daily from noon to 10 p.m, serving a huge variety of burgers (Monterrey, Italian, fried onion). Pricier — cheeseburger single starts at $11.99, the 5.3.2 burger goes up to $28.49.

8. Little Havana Restaurant (12727 Biscayne Blvd): Open weekdays 11:30 a.m. to 9 p.m. and until 10 p.m. weekends. Beef and ham-and-cheese empanadas at $2.49, Cuban sandwiches at $7.99, ropa vieja at $8.99 — budget-friendly Cuban classics.

9. Korean Kitchen (1661 NE 163rd St): Open Monday-Friday 11 a.m.-3 p.m. and 5-8 p.m., Saturday 12 p.m.-9:15 p.m. Signature bibimbap rice bowls (vegetable, spicy squid, pork bulgogi) range $14.00-$16.25, noodles from $14.00, stews from $12.75.

10. World Famous House of Mac (13521 Biscayne Blvd): Open daily from noon, closing 11 p.m. Monday-Saturday and midnight Sundays. Unique mac and cheese flavors including pizza ($15), chicken cordon bleu ($15), jerk shrimp ($20), and a vegan option ($18).
