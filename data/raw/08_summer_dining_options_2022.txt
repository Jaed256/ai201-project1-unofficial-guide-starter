TITLE: On Campus Dining Options for Students this Summer
AUTHORS: Juliana Narvaez & Will Smith
DATE: 2022-06-22

With the slowed down pace of the summer semester, many on-campus dining options have changed their hours of operation to accommodate for the decrease in students on campus. Luckily, there is still a large variety of dining options available for students during Summer B.

Almazar — Graham Center (MMC), Monday-Friday 11 a.m.-4 p.m. Located inside the Graham Center's main food court in between Pollo Tropical and Chili's, Almazar offers classic Middle Eastern and Greek dishes. Wait times for take out are low, good for quality food in between classes.

Barnes & Noble at FIU Café — Graham Center (MMC), Monday-Friday 9 a.m.-6 p.m., Saturday 10 a.m.-2 p.m. On the second floor of GC's Barnes & Noble, offering premium Starbucks drinks and a variety of snacks and meal options, with spacious indoor and outdoor seating.

Café Bustelo — Graham Center (MMC), Monday-Friday 7 a.m.-5 p.m., Saturday 8 a.m.-12 p.m. Offering authentic Latin pastries, desserts, sandwiches, and café Cubano. Located on the first floor of GC in the main food court area — perfect for signature Cuban coffee and pastries.

Chili's Too — Graham Center (MMC), Monday-Thursday 11 a.m.-3 p.m., Friday 11 a.m.-2 p.m. Inside GC next to Almazar, offers food and a full bar, signature Chili's dishes.

Jamba Juice — Graham Center (MMC), Monday-Friday 8 a.m.-1 p.m. On GC's first floor across from Barnes & Noble. Plant-based smoothies, bowls with fresh fruit, and protein-packed food.

La Ventanita — Graham Center (MMC), Monday-Friday 9 a.m.-6 p.m., Saturday 10 a.m.-2 p.m. Open since November 2019, brings Miami culture to FIU students — cortaditos, pastelitos, quick bites and a comfortable outdoor gathering area. Outside GC next to Barnes & Noble.

Panera Bread — Graham Center (MMC), Monday-Friday 10:30 a.m.-4 p.m. Open since 2020, located outside GC in front of the dining hall. Counter-service bakery and cafe items, a warm study spot.

Pollo Tropical — Graham Center (MMC), Monday-Friday 10:30 a.m.-6 p.m. The South Florida-based restaurant is a well known dining spot among FIU students, famous for citrus-marinated grilled chicken. First floor of GC.

RechargeU — Graham Center (MMC), Monday-Friday 9 a.m.-6 p.m., Saturday 10 a.m.-2 p.m. Convenience-style dining option for students in a rush, next to Barnes & Noble.

Subway — Graham Center, Monday-Friday 11 a.m.-5 p.m., Saturday 11 a.m.-3 p.m. First floor of GC next to Sergio's Cuban. Customizable subs, wraps, and salads.

Sushi Maki — Graham Center (MMC), Monday-Thursday 11 a.m.-6 p.m., Friday 11 a.m.-5 p.m. Poke bowls, sushi rolls, and signature Bubble Teas. First floor of GC.

Chick-fil-A — PG5 Market Station (MMC), Monday-Thursday 11 a.m.-5 p.m., Friday 11 a.m.-4 p.m.; Wolfe University Center (BBC), Monday-Thursday 11 a.m.-4 p.m., Friday 11 a.m.-3 p.m. Chicken sandwiches at both MMC and BBC.

Crepe King — PG5 Market Station (MMC), Monday-Friday 11 a.m.-4 p.m. Sweet and savory crepes inside PG5.

Salad Creations — PG5 Market Station (MMC), Monday-Thursday 11 a.m.-3 p.m., Friday 11 a.m.-5 p.m. Chopped salads inside PG5.

Panda Express — MANGO building first floor, Monday-Thursday 11 a.m.-3 p.m., Friday 11 a.m.-2 p.m. Chinese regional cuisine with bold American tastes.

Starbucks — Green Library (MMC), Monday-Thursday 8:30 a.m.-8:30 p.m., Friday 8:30 a.m.-5 p.m.; Glenn Hubert Library (BBC), Monday-Friday 8:30 a.m.-2:30 p.m. Every college student's saving grace during finals week.

Halfmoon Empanadas — PG6 Market Station, Monday-Friday 8 a.m.-4 p.m. Ready-to-order Latin American empanadas with sweet and savory fillings.

Market at Breezeway — University Towers (MMC), Monday-Friday 12 p.m.-9 p.m., Saturday-Sunday 4 p.m.-9 p.m. Convenience-styled, famous Breezeway cookies. One of the few weekend options.

Tropical Smoothie Café — Wellness and Recreation Center (MMC), Monday-Friday 10 a.m.-6 p.m. Smoothies, sandwiches, wraps, salads and flatbreads, with an open study area.
